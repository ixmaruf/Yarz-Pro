About YARZ & Company Policies

YARZ is a 100% online-based Premium Men's Fashion Brand operating from Narayanganj, Dhaka Division, Bangladesh. As a modern direct-to-consumer e-commerce brand, we operate entirely online without a physical retail storefront, ensuring premium quality at the best value. YARZ strictly adheres to all national e-commerce guidelines with fully documented policies.

  • Brand Founder & Creation: YARZ was created and founded by Maruf Hasan in 2026.
  • Shipping Policy: We offer nationwide delivery across Bangladesh. Cash on Delivery (COD) is fully supported. Inside Dhaka delivery takes 2-3 days, and outside Dhaka takes 3-5 days.
  • Return & Refund Policy: We provide a transparent 7-day return and exchange policy for all our premium clothing items to ensure 100% customer satisfaction.
  • Privacy Policy & Terms of Service: YARZ enforces strict data protection. All customer data is secured. We do not sell data to third parties. Secure checkout is guaranteed.
  • Customer Support & Community: Our 24/7 customer support is available via WhatsApp (+8801601743670) and Facebook Messenger. We have clear Community Guidelines for our users.

For full details, users can navigate to the respective policy pages linked in our website footer (/privacy, /terms, /shipping, /return-policy).
