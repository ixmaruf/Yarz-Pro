About YARZ

Premium Men's Fashion from Bangladesh

Our Brand Story

YARZ (yarzclothing.xyz) is a premium Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) men's fashion brand proudly established in Bangladesh. In a market flooded with generic options, YARZ was created to bridge the gap between international quality standards and local accessibility. Our choice of the .xyz domain reflects our modern, Gen-Z, and digital-first identity—we believe true brand value comes from uncompromising product quality and customer experience, rather than traditional domain conventions.

Founder's Vision & Mission

Founded in 2026 by Maruf Hasan, a visionary young entrepreneur from Narayanganj, YARZ started with a simple yet powerful mission: to redefine men's fashion in Bangladesh by offering premium quality clothing that doesn't break the bank. Our mission is to empower every Bangladeshi man to look and feel confident, with apparel that combines timeless elegance with modern comfort.

Why YARZ? (The YARZ Standard)

We are a registered and verified brand committed to 100% transparency. When you choose YARZ, you are choosing authenticity:

  • Proudly Made in Bangladesh — Supporting local artisans and maintaining world-class manufacturing standards.
  • Premium Material Composition — We use a wide variety of high-quality fabrics carefully selected for each specific garment to ensure maximum comfort and longevity.
  • Season-Optimized Fabric GSM — From breathable lightweight weaves for the scorching summer to cozy, thicker fabrics for winter, our GSM is tailored perfectly for the local climate.
  • No-Compromise Quality Control — Every single garment goes through strict, multi-layer quality checks before being dispatched.

Trust & Credibility

Customer trust is the foundation of our brand. We stand behind our products with industry-leading policies:

  • Fair Exchange Policy — We offer a conditional exchange policy for unused and unwashed items, ensuring genuine customers always get the right fit without enabling abuse.
  • Reliable Delivery Timeline — Fast shipping across Bangladesh (Inside Dhaka: 1-2 days, Outside Dhaka: 2-3 days).
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) — Secure and risk-free shopping with nationwide COD options.
  • Secure Data Processing — Our website utilizes state-of-the-art encryption to protect your personal information.

Delivery

We deliver across Bangladesh. Delivery charges are dynamically calculated and displayed at checkout. Due to varying operational conditions of courier services across the country, delivery charges are subject to change from time to time. Cash on Delivery (COD) is available nationwide.
