Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At YARZ, we respect and value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, make a purchase, or interact with us in any other way.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): Name, phone number, delivery address, and (optionally) email address. If you pay via bKash or Nagad Send Money, we also collect the Transaction ID you submit — solely to verify your payment.
  • Non-Personal Information: Browser type, IP address, device information, and other non-identifiable data that helps us improve your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To process, pack, and deliver your orders through our courier partners.
  • To send order confirmations and updates (via phone, WhatsApp, or Messenger — we do not send automated email blasts).
  • To respond to inquiries and complaints, and to provide customer support.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To prevent fraud and fake orders.

3. Payment Information

We collect no credit or debit card numbers and no banking credentials, because YARZ does not use any automated payment gateway (such as Stripe or SSLCommerz). Payment is handled in two ways:

  • Cash on Delivery (COD): You pay the courier in cash, and we never see any financial data.
  • bKash / Nagad Send Money: You send the amount from your own bKash/Nagad to our number 01601-743670 and submit only the Transaction ID at checkout. This ID is used once, to verify your payment manually, and is stored with your order record.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information only as necessary to fulfil your order, with:

  • Courier partners (Pathao, Steadfast, RedX, Sundarban) — only your name, phone, and delivery address.
  • bKash / Nagad — we do not send them anything; you initiate the Send Money yourself.
  • Meta (Facebook / Instagram) — only in hashed (irreversibly encrypted) form for ad attribution via Meta's Conversions API.

These partners are obligated to keep your information confidential and secure.

5. Security of Your Information

Our website runs on HTTPS, and your order data travels to our server in encrypted form. Because we do not accept card payments, your financial data never enters our systems. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security — please contact us immediately if you suspect any issue.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and tracking technologies (Meta Pixel and Conversions API) to enhance your browsing experience and to show you ads about products you viewed. You can control cookie settings through your browser, but disabling cookies may affect some features of our site.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request the deletion of your personal data (subject to record-keeping obligations for completed orders).
  • Opt out of receiving promotional messages at any time by emailing us or messaging us on WhatsApp.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies when visiting them.

9. Children's Privacy

YARZ does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take immediate steps to delete it.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

YARZ may update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page and become effective immediately upon posting. Please review this policy periodically for any updates.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us at:

  • Email: yarzzzbd@gmail.com
  • WhatsApp / Phone: +880 1601 743670

By using our website and services, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
